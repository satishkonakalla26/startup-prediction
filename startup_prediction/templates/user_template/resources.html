📊 Understanding Our Model
Model Specifications:
✓ Algorithm: XGBoost (Gradient Boosting)
✓ Accuracy: 80%+
✓ Features Analyzed: 21
✓ Training Data: Real startup data
✓ Predictions: SUCCESS or FAILURE

The model analyzes funding patterns, business relationships, industry category, and investor participation to predict startup viability.
🔍 What Each Feature Means
  • 📞 Relationships: Number of business contacts and partnerships
  • 💰 Funding Rounds: Number of times funding was raised
  • 💵 Total Funding: Total amount of capital invested (in USD)
  • 💼 Industry Category: Type of business (Software, Web, Mobile, etc.)
  • 🏦 Investor Types: VC, Angel, specific round types (A, B, C, D)
  • 👥 Avg Participants: Average number of participants per funding round
  • ⭐ Top 500: Whether company is in Forbes top 500
